Good to Know

💵Currency: USD widely accepted in Tamarindo. Carry small bills for vendors and tips.
🌧Weather: Green season — brief afternoon showers are normal. Mornings are sunny. Pack a light rain jacket.
🌊Ocean safety: Rip currents exist on Playa Tamarindo. Swim near flags. If caught in a rip — swim parallel to shore.
🧴Sun: Reef-safe sunscreen required near coral. Reapply every 2 hrs — tropical sun burns fast even on cloudy days.
🚕Getting around: Tamarindo is walkable. Taxis are cheap ($3–8 local). Ask the hotel for trusted drivers.
💰Tipping: 10% is often included on restaurant bills. Tip tour guides $5–10/person — it's meaningful.
